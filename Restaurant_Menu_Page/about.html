History of our Fast Food

One fascinating fact about pasta is its ancient origins. While commonly associated with Italy, pasta's history traces back much further. The earliest known reference to pasta dates back to the 1st century AD in the writings of the Roman poet Horace. However, pasta-like dishes were likely consumed even earlier by ancient civilizations such as the Etruscans and Greeks. Additionally, in 1279, a will drafted in the town of Genoa, Italy, mentioned dried pasta among the deceased's belongings, providing evidence of pasta consumption in medieval Italy. This long history illustrates pasta's enduring presence across cultures and its evolution into the beloved staple it is today.
